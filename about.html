My Story

I started Vireon Marketing as an independent digital agency focused on one thing: making every ad naira count. What began as a passion for understanding why some campaigns explode and others die quietly evolved into a full practice built around Meta Ads strategy, creative content, and the psychology of the Nigerian consumer.

My marketing philosophy is grounded in the principles of Akin Alabi — fear-based messaging, irresistible offers, storytelling over feature lists, and the discipline of building trust before asking for the sale. These aren't theories to me. They're things I test every week inside Ads Manager.

Studying COS 106 at MIVA Open University is part of a deliberate push to add technical depth to my marketing work — because the best marketers understand the technology their campaigns run on.
